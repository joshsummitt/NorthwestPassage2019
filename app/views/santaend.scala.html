You found Santa!

You have been hopelessly lost in the frozen North for months. Out of supplies and near death you hear a jingle in the distance. You look out on the frozen expanse and see a growing red light on the horizon. As it approaches an image comes into focus. You can't believe your eyes. It's Santa Claus on a training route. Christmas has come early this year. You didn't make it to China but you did make it home safely thanks to a timely ride from ole St. Nick himself.
